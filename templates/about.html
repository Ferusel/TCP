The Crock Pot
About The Website
What's this website about?

This website is a webcrawler for food recipes, scrapped from three websites (see footer). Somtimes, it's pretty inconvenient to find a recipe, only to learn you don't have all the ingredients for it. Using this website, you can specify recipes for ingredients that you do have, which makes things more convenient!

Disclaimer: I do NOT own any recipes you find here. As mentioned, I scrapped them from the websites. All recipes belong to the owners that wrote them!

Where did this come from?

The Crock Pot is a project that I embarked on after learning very basic web development from Harvard's CS50 Course, and acts as my final project for the course. As countries worldwide entered various stages of lockdown during the COVID-19 Pandemic, carrying out daily routines such as cooking and grocery shopping became increasingly difficult and expensive. I realized there was a need to maximize and stretch the food we had at home, but I found it troublesome to trawl through recipe after recipe to find one that I could actually cook, thus The Crock Pot was born.

Although still in its early stages, I do think this website has potential to be something. As I continue improving on its functions and (hopefully) not get sued by the websites I'm scraping from, I hope to create something that will be useful to everyone, as we continue battling against the COVID-19 Pandemic (and food waste in general!).

As I was researching on this problem, I came across this reddit post that highlighted the problem I mentioned, but at the extremes. I hope things improve soon.

Hey, your website sucks!

Owing to my general inexperience at coding, with The Crock Pot being my first serious project learning code from the ground-up, I recognize I have much to learn. Yes, as of writing, my website sucks. In fact, I don't think anyone would even use this thing. I don't even know why I'm writing this About page (but if you're here, I'm grateful for the company).

Nevertheless, I will steadily improve my skills, and I hope to improve upon The Crock Pot steadily. If you wish to criticize my work and help me improve, you can do so here. I gladly appreciate it!
